About Luna Silvana!

My name is Luna Silvana, but you can call me Lunes! I am a 16 year old trans girl with Asperger's Syndrome. This website is for my personal blog and overall homepage of my internet prescence.

I believe that people should be as independent as possible. This website is an example of how easy it is to become independent from social media.

I'd be a hypocrite to say it isn't useful, however. Mainstream social media, though, should be avoided.
